The broken website detection still produces too many false positives.

Completely redesign the validation logic.

The goal is NOT to detect websites that bots cannot load.

The goal is to detect websites that a normal human user would consider truly broken.

Accuracy is more important than speed.

Increase timeout to:

15 seconds

Many legitimate business websites take 2-8 seconds to load.

Do not classify a website as broken simply because it is slow.

Step 1:

Try:

https://domain.com

Wait up to 15 seconds.

Step 2:

If failed:

Try again.

Wait another 15 seconds.

Step 3:

If failed:

Try:

http://domain.com

Wait up to 15 seconds.

Step 4:

If failed:

Open the website in a completely new browser context.

Wait another 15 seconds.

A website should NEVER be classified as broken after only one attempt.
